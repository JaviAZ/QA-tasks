As an estate agent, I want to predict the price of a given house, so that I
can market it at a good price.

As an estate agent, I want to add homes to the application, so that
customers can see them.

As a customer, I want to browse the selection of homes, so that I can rent
or buy.

As a customer, I want to know how to contact the letting agent, so that I
can arrange a viewing.

As a clerk, I want to access tenant records, so that I can audit stored data.
